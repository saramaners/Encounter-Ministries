Testimonies

These are the stories of those whose spiritual lives have been impacted by Encounter.

"I felt welcome and accepted."
"For years I've gone to church, mindlessly, aimlessly, never feeling worthy of the calling. Encounter (which is NOT a church, but a student-led worship, sponsored by Skyline) is wholly different. I'm the same stroke, I felt welcomed and accepted, but also compelled to change my life; not out of fear of the judgement of others, but out of a newly ignited desire to follow Christ. Encounter is not perfect: it's led by students, so it's always a little messy. But Jesus called upon people with messy lives! He came to heal a broken world, and Encounter provides an opportunity for all people to come and be filled with grace and love. Unfortunately, I was only able to briefly be a part of this incredible ministry, but I know God has chosen Henderson to pour out His Spirit and work wonders." -Eric Winningham
"God's Spirit is Moving in Henderson!"
"Encounter has been such a blessing in my life. God's Spirit is moving in Henderson! Please pray that we can reach those who do not know Jesus and that God will use us to expand the boundaries of the Kingdom of Heaven." -Nolan Huber
"God showed me... redemption through the hearts of our servant leaders."
"Just one year ago this place was still just a dream and God has brought that dream into reality. Tonight as our servant leaders gathered to pray and talk about this upcoming semester God showed me His power and His loving story of redemption though the hearts of our servant leaders. So excited to serve along side some wonderful people as we begin a journey partnering along side God and His mission in the city of Henderson." -William Murphy
