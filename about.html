About Us

Encounter Ministries is a student-run Wednesday night worship based in Henderson, TN. This group came together to encourage each other, to encounter Jesus through authentic worship, prayer, a loving community, and loving our community. For more information on how to get involved, message us on Facebook or email Nolan Huber. Join us this Wednesday at 116 E Main St. in downtown Henderson.
